Target Audience

This website is directed toward: Individuals, Professionals, Transportation Professionals, Travellers, Event Planners, etc.

Age: All Ages

Income: As this website is built primarily as a source of information income should not be a factor regarding our audience.

Personas

Bob D. Baker

Occupation: Baker

Demographic: Bob is in his late 40s and owns a small bakery in an affluent area.

Education: Bob has little formal education, apprenticed under an experienced baker for many years.

Technological Experience: Personal computers, smart phones. Prefers larger screens and tablets.

Motivation: Bob is an expert in his craft, and he plans his bakes based on temperature and atmospheric pressure.

Harriet Handegraf

Occupation: Artist

Demographic: Mid-Twenties, Metropolitan

Education: Studied at various prestigious art academies, bachelors in art education, masters in art theory.

Technological Experience: Owns only a smart phone.

Motivation: Harriet builds many outdoor art displays, many of which can be affected by the weather. She needs to know the weather to plan future and current shows.
