I Feel Like Adding Content is Busy Work

Ben Smith

I've never been asked to write my own copy. I'm a software engineer, not a copywriter. I suppose I could write a consumer that grabs random articles, but then I have to deal with the legalities of finding material that won't get me in trouble. So, here I am, typing away when I'd rather be spending time with my family.

This is about all I've got.

Sometimes I think these classes are made to ruin my day. I'd like to go enjoy the nice weather outside, but after 50hr work weeks, 20-30 hours of school, there really is no time. So, I don't know what the weather is outside. I think it might be cold. If you really want to know what the weather is like outside, check the site. I'd love to go hiking, or spend some time with my family, but I have a 2 credit religion class that asks me to do 3 hours of reading every day. I don't have that time.
