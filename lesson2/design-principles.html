White Space and Clean Design

Swappa

Swappa.com

Swappa creates clear space between objects and input areas, creating a very clean UI. Each event area is easily distinguished and spaced so that fat fingers will not accidentally fumble. Eyes are drawn to specific elements due to proper spacing.

Hick-Hyman Law

Airbnb

Airbnb.com

Airbnb leaves little choice in your options for action. You are either going to tell them where you are going, tell them you don't care where you are going, or going to check on an item you are specifically there to see (Inbox, profile, etc.)

PARC: Repetition

Amazon

Amazon.com
Amazon design is consistent throughout, the UI does not change, the menu does not change. You know where you are at all times.
